The Bio of Al Aganya

by Al Aganya

About me

There's not much to know about me since I've only been alive for a short while.
Nevertheless, I think the little there is is quite interesting.
I was born Seventeen years ago right here in the capital, Nairobi.
Needless to say it was a joyous occasion for my parents, I'm the first of two.
I started education at Le pic primary school and was there until Class 5.
I then moved to Kenvic school where I completed primary school education.
Afterwards, I joined Starehe Boys' Centre and School for my four years of high school.
I am currently at Moringa school studying programming.

Reasons for choosing programming

I started programming because I wanted to develop hands-on skills
relevant to the current tech migration of Kenya and the world in general.
I've also been passionate about everything computer-oriented since I knew what the word meant.
For this reason I decided that programming would best suit my pursuit for more and more knowledge on computers.
